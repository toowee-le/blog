A little bit about me

🌝 Hey, I'm Tuyet, or Toowee. I'm a British born Vietnamese girl on a journey of learning to code alongside my charity job.


I'm passionate about everything web development. I don't have a CS background, in fact I graduated in Psychology and Sociology! My interest in tech sparked as soon as I got my first computer and dabbled in HTML to design websites on Piczo at aged 13.


I never considered pursuing web development as a potential career but after speaking with my developer friends and being introduced to the tech space with the Code First: Girls community, I realised I wanted to take this seriously.


In my spare time, you'll see me weight-lifting, playing animal crossing, planning my next holiday or Netflix binging.
