A little bit about me

🌝 Hey, I'm Tuyet, or Toowee. I'm a British born Vietnamese girl on a journey of learning to code.


I don't have a CS background, in fact I graduated in Psychology and Sociology! My love of tech sparked when I got my first computer at 12 and designed Piczo sites in HTML and CSS.


I never thought turning my interest of creating websites could be a potential career but after speaking with my developer friends and being introduced to the vibrant tech space including Code First: Girls, codebar and the #100DaysOfCode community, this inspired me to take learning to code seriously to make the transition into tech.


In my spare time, you'll see me reading, weight-lifting, playing animal crossing, planning my next holiday, obsessing over cat gifs or Netflix binging 🏋 🎮 📕 🏝 🎥.
